Research Overview

My current research is within the domain of Medical CPS, with an emphasis on potentially detecting the onset of advanced heart-failure (HF) from the analysis of clinical metrics such as Pulse-Wave Velocity (PWV). Early detection of HF is essential in slowing the progression of the condition and even potentially reversing with treatment. I would like to do additional work in the domain of Automotive CPS, particularly in developing passenger-safe end-to-end deep-learning frameworks for autonomous vehicles which convert camera inputs into mechanical control actions.
